Our Story

Pattie's Jewellery - Since 1985

Located in the vibrant Queensway House on Kaunda Street in Nairobi Central, Pattie's Jewellery has been crafting exquisite jewelry pieces for over three decades.

We specialize in both retail and manufacturing, offering a wide range of jewelry from classic designs to contemporary pieces. Each item is crafted with meticulous attention to detail and quality.

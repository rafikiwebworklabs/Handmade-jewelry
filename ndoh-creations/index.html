Our Story

Ndoh Creations was founded with a passion for creating unique, handcrafted jewelry that tells a story. Each piece is meticulously designed and crafted by our skilled artisans in Nairobi.

We take pride in using ethically sourced materials and traditional techniques to create jewelry that stands the test of time while celebrating African heritage.
